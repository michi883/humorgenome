    Humor Genome, Explained

    Start by picking the vibe that sets your meme’s stage, from reaction shots to surreal visuals. Layer in a humor style—lighthearted, nerdy, chaotic, and beyond—then dial the cleverness to choose how dense the punchline should be. The lab spins up two meme drafts using your genome so you can compare expressions of the same comedic DNA.

    Roll new matchups, vote on the one that lands hardest, and watch the feedback stream capture each decision. Every pair you evaluate helps refine which combinations resonate and why. When you spot a meme you love, download it, remix it, and keep the experiment running.
